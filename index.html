BioFusion Trash‑to‑Resource Reactor (BTR²)

On Mars, nothing is trash.

Summary

BioFusion BTR² is a hybrid recycling concept that fuses synthetic biology, nanotechnology, and AI to transform inorganic mission waste into usable feedstock for 3D printing, repairs, and construction on Mars.

How It Works

  1. BioReactor: Engineered extremophiles target plastics/textiles.
  2. NanoDisassembly: Mechano‑chemical separation of metals/ceramics.
  3. AI Manager: Predicts demand, schedules cycles, monitors safety.
